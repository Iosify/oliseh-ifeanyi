About Me

The mind behind the work

"I am a professional overthinker with a sketchbook and a scientific mind."

My journey spans four interconnected worlds: the precision of physics, the emotion of poetry, the creativity of graphic design, and the rigour of quality assurance.

By day, I navigate equations, research, and QA processes. By night, I break those rules with poetry, design, and food art. This multiplicity isn't a contradiction, it's the essence of who I am.

I believe true innovation happens when disciplines collide, and my work lives in those intersections.
